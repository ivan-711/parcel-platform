# Parcel Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 6, 2026

Parcel ("Parcel," "we," "us," or "our") runs parceldesk.io and the Parcel real estate deal analysis platform. This Privacy Policy explains what we collect, how we use it, who we share it with, how long we keep it, and what choices you have.

This policy is written for the product as it exists today. Parcel helps users analyze deals, manage property and contact records, run skip traces, send direct mail, upload documents, and generate AI-assisted deal analysis. We try to follow practical FTC small-business guidance: collect only what we need, limit access, protect what we keep, and delete or de-identify data when we no longer need it.

If you do not agree with this policy, do not use Parcel.

## 1. What Parcel collects

### Information you give us

- Account information, such as your name, email address, password, role, team information, and subscription plan details.
- Authentication information if you sign in through Clerk, such as your Clerk user ID and the profile data Clerk provides to us.
- Optional contact information you choose to provide, such as a phone number through authentication, support, or communications features.
- Property and deal information you enter or save, such as addresses, city/state/ZIP, tax and valuation data, strategy assumptions, notes, contacts, tasks, reports, and pipeline records.
- Contact and outreach information, such as owner names, email addresses, phone numbers, mailing addresses, notes, tags, direct mail templates, and communication history.
- Skip tracing request data and results, including address inputs, owner names, phone numbers, email addresses, mailing addresses, absentee-owner flags, and related lookup metadata.
- Documents you upload, including PDFs, DOCX files, and images, plus extracted text, AI summaries, key terms, risk flags, extracted figures, search chunks, and embeddings.
- Communications content when you use Parcel to send or receive SMS or email, including message bodies, subjects, delivery status, reply content, and related metadata.
- Messages you send to support or feedback you provide to us.

### Information we collect automatically

- Authentication and security data, such as session cookies, refresh cookies, login events, IP address, browser type, device information, and request logs.
- Usage and product analytics, such as page views, feature usage, event logs, and user identifiers used to understand how the product is used.
- Public report viewing data when a report is shared by link, such as a hashed IP, user agent, referrer, sections viewed, and time spent.
- Error and performance data needed to monitor uptime, security, and reliability.

### Information from third parties and public sources

- Property and public-record style data from providers such as RentCast and, if enabled for a feature, Bricked.
- Skip tracing and owner contact data from BatchData.
- Address verification and mail delivery status from Lob.
- Subscription and billing status information from Stripe. Parcel does not store full payment card numbers.
- Authentication profile data from Clerk.

## 2. How we use your information

We use the information above to:

- Create and secure your account.
- Authenticate you and keep you signed in.
- Provide deal analysis, property enrichment, skip tracing, direct mail, document processing, AI chat, reporting, and other product features.
- Store and retrieve your uploaded files and generated outputs.
- Send transactional messages, such as password resets, document-ready notifications, and billing-related notices.
- Operate communications features, including sending and receiving SMS and email if you choose to use those tools.
- Measure usage, improve the product, troubleshoot bugs, monitor performance, and detect abuse or fraud.
- Enforce our Terms of Service and maintain audit trails for billing, compliance, and security.
- Comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and protect users, Parcel, and the public.

We do not sell personal information for money. We also do not use your personal information for third-party behavioral advertising.

- If you use shared-report links, anyone with the link may be able to view the report until you turn sharing off or revoke the link.
- If you use direct mail, communications, or other outreach features, the data needed to complete that request will be shared with the provider you chose to use.

### Legal and business disclosures

We may disclose information:

- To comply with law, regulation, court order, subpoena, or lawful government request.
- To investigate fraud, security incidents, harassment, misuse, or violations of our Terms.
- To protect the rights, safety, or property of Parcel, our users, or others.
- In connection with a merger, acquisition, financing, restructuring, bankruptcy, or sale of all or part of our business.

## 4. Cookies and similar technologies

Parcel uses cookies and similar technologies for a few core purposes:

- To keep you signed in and protect your session.
- To secure login, prevent abuse, and support authentication through Clerk or our own auth system.
- To remember product preferences.
- To understand product usage and improve the service.
- To support billing and account flows when you interact with Stripe or other service providers.

Some cookies are necessary for Parcel to work. Others, such as analytics cookies, help us understand product usage. You can control cookies through your browser settings, but blocking required cookies may break sign-in or other core features.

## 5. Data retention

We keep information for as long as we need it to run Parcel, comply with law, resolve disputes, prevent fraud, and enforce our agreements. In practice:

- Account and workspace data are generally kept while your account is active.
- Documents, deals, contacts, skip trace results, direct mail records, and communications are generally kept until you delete them, ask us to delete them, or we no longer need them for service, security, or compliance reasons.
- Security, troubleshooting, and analytics logs are generally kept only as long as operationally necessary.
- Billing, tax, payment-dispute, and similar financial records may be kept longer when required by law or accounting rules.
- Backup copies may remain for a limited period after deletion and are then overwritten or removed in the normal backup cycle.

If you ask us to delete your account, we will delete or de-identify data we no longer need, but we may keep limited records for billing, security, fraud prevention, legal compliance, or dispute resolution.

## 6. Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards designed to protect the information we hold. These include access controls, HTTPS/TLS in transit, signed file access for stored documents, provider-managed infrastructure controls, and logging for security and fraud prevention.

No system is completely secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security, and you should also protect your own account credentials and devices.

## 7. Your choices and rights

You can ask us to:

- Confirm whether we have personal information about you.
- Give you access to the information we hold about you.
- Correct inaccurate account or profile information.
- Delete your account or certain data, subject to legal and operational exceptions.

You can also:

- Update profile information inside Parcel where that feature is available.
- Delete individual uploaded documents from within the product.
- Turn email notifications on or off in settings.
- Revoke public report sharing by disabling or deleting the shared report.

To make a privacy request, email support@parceldesk.io with "Privacy Request" in the subject line. We may ask you to verify your identity before we act on the request.

## 8. California residents

Parcel extends the following privacy rights to California residents:

- The right to know what categories of personal information we collect, use, disclose, and retain.
- The right to request access to specific pieces of personal information, subject to verification and legal exceptions.
- The right to request deletion of personal information we collected from you, subject to exceptions.
- The right to request correction of inaccurate personal information we maintain about you.
- The right to know whether we sell or share personal information. Parcel does not sell personal information for money and does not share personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising.
- The right not to be discriminated against for exercising privacy rights.

California residents may submit requests by emailing support@parceldesk.io. If you have an account with us, submitting the request from the email tied to your account will help us verify it faster. We may still need more information to verify your identity.
