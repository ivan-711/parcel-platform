# Parcel Compliance Copy

Effective Date: April 6, 2026

These are production-ready UI copy snippets for Parcel's current compliance gaps.

## 1. Skip tracing page

### Checkbox label

I certify this skip trace is for a lawful business purpose. I will not use Parcel or skip-traced data to stalk, harass, threaten, discriminate against, or unlawfully contact anyone, and I will not use the data for any purpose prohibited by the FCRA, TCPA, CAN-SPAM, do-not-call rules, or applicable state privacy or solicitation laws.

### Helper text

Parcel records this acknowledgment with your trace request.

### Validation error

You must confirm lawful use before running a skip trace.

## 2. Direct mail page

### Checkbox label

I confirm this mail campaign is truthful, non-deceptive, and lawful. I am responsible for complying with applicable privacy, consumer protection, fair housing, real estate marketing, and state wholesaling or disclosure laws, and I will not use Parcel or Lob to harass, threaten, mislead, or discriminate against any person.

### Helper text

You are responsible for the recipient list, the content you send, and any state-specific disclosures your campaign requires.

### Validation error

You must confirm compliance before sending direct mail.

## 3. Registration

### Checkbox label

I agree to Parcel's Terms of Service and Privacy Policy.

### Validation error

You must accept the Terms of Service and Privacy Policy to create an account.

## 4. Delete my account flow

### Dialog title

Delete your account?

### Dialog body

Deleting your account permanently removes your Parcel login and your workspace data, including saved deals, contacts, uploaded documents, skip traces, mail campaigns, and settings. This action cannot be undone. We may keep limited records when needed for billing, fraud prevention, security, legal compliance, or dispute resolution.

### Optional paid-plan notice

If you have an active subscription, future renewals will stop, but already-billed monthly fees are not refunded. Annual plans receive a prorated refund under our Terms of Service.

### Confirmation checkbox

I understand this action is permanent.

### Primary button

Delete my account

### Secondary button

Keep my account

### Success message

Your account deletion request has been received. We will delete or de-identify the data we no longer need and keep only the limited records required for billing, security, fraud prevention, legal compliance, or dispute resolution.
