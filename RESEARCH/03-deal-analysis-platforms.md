## 1. DealCheck

**URL:** [dealcheck.io](https://dealcheck.io)
**Category:** Multi-strategy deal analysis platform
**User Base:** 350,000+ investors and agents

### Pricing

| Plan | Monthly (billed annually) | Limits |
|------|--------------------------|--------|
| **Starter** | Free | 15 saved properties, basic analysis |
| **Plus** | $10/mo | 50 saved properties, 15 photos, 10 sales/rental comps, 10 templates, offer calculator |
| **Pro** | $20/mo | Unlimited properties/photos/comps/templates, white-labeled reports, CRM, CSV export |

Both paid plans include a 14-day free trial.

### Calculator Types

- **Rental Property Calculator** -- long-term buy-and-hold analysis
- **BRRRR Calculator** -- buy, rehab, rent, refinance, repeat
- **Fix & Flip Calculator** -- purchase-through-sale profit analysis
- **Multi-Family & Commercial Calculator** -- apartment buildings, mixed-use, commercial
- **Wholesaling Calculator** -- assignment fee, MAO, buyer strategy targeting
- **Airbnb / Short-Term Rental** -- handled via rental and BRRRR calculators with STR inputs

### Financial Metrics Calculated

- Cap rate (purchase and pro forma)
- Cash-on-cash return (CoC)
- Return on investment (ROI)
- Return on equity (ROE)
- Internal rate of return (IRR) -- full time-value-of-money calculation
- Gross rent multiplier (GRM)
- Break-even ratio (BER)
- Debt coverage ratio (DCR / DSCR)
- Rent-to-value ratio
- Net operating income (NOI)
- Cash flow (monthly and annual)
- Equity accumulation
- Total profit on sale

### Multi-Year Projections

- **Up to 35 years** of buy-and-hold projections
- Default display: Years 1, 2, 3, 5, 10, 20, 30 (customizable)
- Projects: cash flow, investment returns, equity accumulation, tax deductions
- Sale analysis for each year: cumulative cash flow, profit, ROI, IRR
- Customizable annual appreciation rate and rent/expense escalation rates

### Tax & Depreciation

- Customizable depreciation period and land value percentage
- Depreciation tax deductions projected over hold period
- Can toggle depreciation on/off
- No explicit 1031 exchange modeling or capital gains tax calculator

### Sensitivity Analysis / Scenario Modeling

- Scenario comparison: duplicate a property and adjust variables, then compare side-by-side
- Adjustable appreciation, rent growth, and expense increase rates
- Offer calculator uses reverse valuation with 12+ customizable criteria
- No dedicated sensitivity analysis tool (e.g., tornado chart or variable sweep)

### Creative Finance Support

- **Up to 5 purchase loans** per property (HELOC, seller financing, private money, hard money)
- **Seller financing:** direct input of seller loan terms, balloon payment support (separate loan term vs. amortization period)
- **Subject-to financing:** input existing loan balance, original rate, remaining term -- DealCheck calculates payments and remaining balance going forward
- **Up to 5 refinance loans** for rental/BRRRR deals, specify year and replacement strategy
- Adjustable compound intervals (monthly, quarterly, semi-annually)

### PDF Reports

4 report types:
1. **Complete Report** -- all info including description, maps, analysis, projections, comps, photos
2. **One-Page Summary** -- single-page property overview
3. **Sales Comps Report** -- list and map of sales comps with ARV estimates
4. **Rental Comps Report** -- list and map of rental comps with gross rent estimates

Report customization (Pro): custom branding, logo, colors, contact info, white-label (hide DealCheck branding). Toggle sections on/off.

### Property Data & Comps

- Import from public records and active MLS listings (no MLS license required)
- Imports: property features, estimated market value, potential rent, listing price, sale history, assessed values
- Up to 20 recent sales & rental comps per property
- Instant ARV and rent estimates from comps
- Owner lookup for off-market leads
- CSV import supported

### Comparison Features

- **Side-by-side comparison** of 2+ properties showing purchase, rehab, financing, cash flow, profit, and return metrics
- Compare different scenarios for the same property (e.g., with vs. without financing)

### Integrations

- iOS and Android mobile apps (full feature parity with web)
- No public API
- CSV/Excel export (Pro)
- Built-in CRM for managing leads and contacts
- Nationwide lender directory

### Strengths vs. Parcel

- Deepest multi-strategy calculator on the market
- 35-year projections with sale analysis each year
- Up to 5 loans per property (creative finance leader)
- Subject-to and seller financing explicitly supported
- Side-by-side property comparison
- Offer calculator with reverse valuation / MAO
- Very strong mobile apps

### Weaknesses

- No public API or Zapier integration
- No dedicated sensitivity analysis (tornado charts, monte carlo)
- No 1031 exchange or capital gains tax modeling
- Limited market-level data (no heatmaps, neighborhood scores)
- No Airbnb-specific data (occupancy, ADR) -- user must input manually

## 8. CrowdStreet

**URL:** [crowdstreet.com](https://crowdstreet.com)
**Category:** Commercial real estate crowdfunding marketplace
**Accredited investors only.**

### Pricing / Minimums

- **Individual deals:** $25,000-$100,000 minimum
- **CrowdStreet Blended Portfolio:** varies
- **Private Managed Accounts:** $250,000 minimum
- Advisory fees: 0.25%-2.5% over investment lifetime (avg ~0.7%/yr)
- Sponsor compensation via promote/profit fee (varies per deal)

### Platform Features

**Investment Types:**
- Individual deals (equity, debt, hybrid)
- Diversified funds
- Managed portfolios
- 732 deals launched from 337 sponsors across 17 property types in 44 states

**Property Types:** Multifamily, retail, office, medical, self-storage, industrial, land

**Deal Analysis (Sponsor-Provided):**
Each listing includes:
- Exhaustive financial analysis and pro forma
- Business plan details
- Market insights and property/location details
- Sponsor profile and track record
- Underwriting assumptions (rental rates, lease expirations, occupancy, exit cap rate)

**Screening Process:**
- FINRA-licensed screening team
- Multi-layered evaluation using Green Street and CoStar data
- Scrutiny of financial reports, rental forecasts, underwriting assumptions, legal documentation

### Financial Metrics Provided

- Target IRR (reported 19.7% realized across portfolio)
- Average cash-on-cash return
- Target equity multiple
- Preferred return structure
- Distribution schedules (typically quarterly)

### Important Context: Nightingale Controversy

In 2023, CrowdStreet faced a major scandal when sponsor Nightingale Properties allegedly misappropriated $63M+ from 800+ investors across two deals. The CEO was criminally charged with wire fraud and pled guilty. CrowdStreet is funding Chapter 11 bankruptcy proceedings. 125 investors sued CrowdStreet for $7.25M in 2025. This significantly impacted platform trust.

### Comparison to Parcel

CrowdStreet is an investment marketplace, not a deal analysis tool. Investors rely on sponsor-provided pro formas rather than running their own analysis. No calculator tools for retail investors.

## Key Takeaways for Parcel

### 1. Parcel's Direct Competitor is DealCheck

DealCheck is the gold standard for multi-strategy deal analysis. With 350K+ users, 6 calculator types, 35-year projections, up to 5 loans per property, seller financing, subject-to, and comprehensive PDF reports, it is the platform Parcel must benchmark against. Key areas to match or exceed:

- **35-year projections** with yearly sale analysis (IRR, cumulative profit)
- **Up to 5 purchase loans** for creative finance scenarios
- **Subject-to and seller financing** with balloon payment support
- **Refinance modeling** (up to 5 future refinances)
- **Offer calculator** with reverse valuation / MAO
- **Side-by-side property comparison**
- **4 PDF report types** including white-label

### 2. Biggest Gaps in the Market (Opportunities for Parcel)

No platform currently offers:

- **NPV calculations** -- none of the major platforms calculate Net Present Value
- **1031 exchange modeling** -- no calculator walks through tax-deferred exchange scenarios
- **Capital gains tax estimation** -- no platform estimates after-tax proceeds on sale
- **True sensitivity analysis** -- tornado charts, variable sweeps, monte carlo simulations
- **AI-powered scenario generation** -- auto-generate best/worst/base case scenarios
- **Cost segregation / bonus depreciation modeling** -- huge tax benefit, no calculator supports it

### 3. Market Positioning Opportunities

| Position | Approach |
|----------|----------|
| **"DealCheck + Tax Intelligence"** | Add 1031 exchange modeling, capital gains estimation, cost segregation, depreciation scheduling to existing calculators |
| **"Calculator + Market Data"** | Combine DealCheck-depth calculators with Mashvisor-style heatmaps and Privy-style deal sourcing |
| **"AI-First Analysis"** | Auto-populate from address, generate scenarios, flag risks, suggest offer price |
| **"All Strategies, One Platform"** | Consolidate rental, BRRRR, flip, wholesale, creative finance, STR, and syndication analysis |

### 4. Feature Priority Recommendations

**Must-Have (Match DealCheck):**
- Multi-year projections (minimum 30 years, ideally 35)
- IRR, DSCR, GRM, BER calculations
- Multiple loan support (at least 3-5 per deal)
- Subject-to and seller financing inputs
- Refinance modeling
- Side-by-side deal comparison
- PDF reports with branding/white-label
- Property data import (public records + MLS)
- Offer calculator / MAO

**Differentiators (Beat DealCheck):**
- NPV calculation
- 1031 exchange scenario modeling
- Capital gains + depreciation recapture tax estimation
- True sensitivity analysis with visualizations
- Airbnb/STR data integration (ADR, occupancy, RevPAR)
- AI-powered auto-analysis from property address
- Interactive charts and visualizations
- Cost segregation / bonus depreciation modeling
- Heatmap or market-level data

### 5. Pricing Benchmark

| Platform | Free Tier | Entry Paid | Full Featured |
|----------|-----------|------------|---------------|
| DealCheck | 15 properties | $10/mo | $20/mo |

DealCheck's $10-20/mo pricing is aggressive. Parcel could differentiate on value at a similar price point, or command a premium ($25-50/mo) by including features no one else offers (tax modeling, sensitivity analysis, STR data).
